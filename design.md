# The Ignorant Yogi — Design System

## Concept
Clean, minimal, slightly mystical. Looks like a serious spiritual website — the joke is entirely in the copy. No winking design elements. Treat Swami J with complete reverence visually.

## Colors
- Background: `#FAF7F2` — warm cream (almost white)
- Surface: `#FFFFFF`
- Text Primary: `#1A1A1A` — near black
- Text Secondary: `#5A5047` — warm charcoal
- Accent: `#C87941` — saffron/burnt orange (the guru color)
- Accent Light: `#F5E6D3` — pale saffron for backgrounds
- Divider: `#E8DDD0`
- Gold: `#B8963E` — for decorative elements, borders

## Typography
- Display / Headings: `Cormorant Garamond` (400, 600) — regal, ancient, slightly theatrical
- Body / UI: `Jost` (300, 400, 500) — clean, modern contrast
- Import via Google Fonts

## Spacing & Layout
- Max content width: 900px centered
- Generous vertical padding between sections (80–120px)
- Minimal horizontal clutter — single column for most content
- Section dividers: thin gold rule or ornamental glyph (✦ or ॐ)

## Components
- Buttons: pill shape, saffron fill, white text, no border-radius overkill
- Cards: white, subtle warm shadow, no rounded-corner excess
- Product cards: minimal — image top, title, status badge (SOLD OUT / ON BACKORDER in small caps)
- Q&A items: question in Cormorant italic, answer in Jost — subtle left gold border

## Motion
- Page load: gentle fade-in stagger on sections
- Store badges: static — no animation needed
- Keep it meditative, not hyperactive

## Tone
The design is earnest and spiritual. The copy is where all the comedy lives.
